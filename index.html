Can we make language models follow behavioral rules without altering weights?

Large language models have emerged as powerful tools for various applications, but controlling their behavior remains challenging. While existing approaches often require model retraining or weight modifications, we introduce Conditional Activation Steering (CAS) - a method that enables real-time, rule-based control of model outputs without changing the model weights.
